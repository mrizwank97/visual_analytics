How quick is it to find Job in EU?

For students about to graduate, the important thing is to find job as soon as possible. So they will be open to move to any country that offers job as soon as possible. The graph on the left shows the time it take to find the job in EU country. The two highlighted countries are Netherlands and Switzerland. In both countries about 75% of foreign people find job within 3 months. This chart shows the facts for year 2021.
